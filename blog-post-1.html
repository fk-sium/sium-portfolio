How I prepared for IELTS — practical tips & routine

Published: November 10, 2025 — by Md. Fazlul Karim Sium

Overview

This post explains my study plan, daily routines, and the materials that helped me achieve my desired band in IELTS.

Daily routine

  • Reading: 30–45 minutes of academic articles & timed practice passages.
  • Listening: 30 minutes of focused listening — podcasts or past papers.
  • Writing: 3 tasks per week (Task 1 + Task 2 practice) with peer or tutor feedback.
  • Speaking: Daily 15–20 minute speaking practice with mock tests or recording yourself.

Resources

I used a mix of official Cambridge books, online practice tests, and active correction from a teacher. Practice under exam conditions frequently.

Final advice

Track progress, focus on weaknesses, and build exam stamina. Structured feedback is the single quickest way to improve.
